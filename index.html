Get to Know the Owners

Meet the Velters

Leo & Jolene Velter
Owners, Satellite Home Watch

Satellite Home Watch is proudly owned and operated by Leo and Jolene Velter. As a family-owned company, we believe that protecting someone's home requires the same level of care and attention we would give our own.

The idea for Satellite Home Watch started after Jolene experienced firsthand the challenges her snowbird parents faced when their property was being monitored by unreliable services while they were away. Missed inspections, poor communication, and small problems turning into expensive repairs.

Satellite Home Watch was created to solve that problem — by combining professional home watch services with a modern client portal that gives homeowners real-time visibility into what's happening at their property.

When you work with Satellite, you're not just hiring a service — you're trusting a family that takes that responsibility seriously.
