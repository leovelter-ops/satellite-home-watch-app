How Service Requests Work · Satellite Home Watch

Submit requests for inspections, vendor visits, or property concerns directly through this page. Our team reviews every request and schedules the appropriate action.

Typical response time: within 12 hours during operations.

What happens after you submit

  • Your team is notified immediately and your request is logged.
  • A coordinator reviews the details and confirms scheduling.
  • You'll receive a confirmation message with your visit details.
  • After the visit, a report is posted to your Documents page.
